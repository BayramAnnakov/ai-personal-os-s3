# Homework prompts — finish Robin's birth certificate

After Workshop 1, you have **3 files** in `~/.claude/` (or `~/.codex/`) generated by `/init-robin` from your conversation history:

- `CLAUDE.md` (or `AGENTS.md` on Codex)
- `SOUL.md`
- `user-profile.md`

Three more files complete Robin's birth certificate. **These can't be inferred from conversations — they require you to declare them.** Each takes ~5 minutes.

Total time: **~15-20 minutes.**

---

## 1 · course-goals.md (5 min)

Paste this into your assistant:

```
I'm taking the AI Personal OS course (5 workshops, May 24 → Jul 19, 2026).

Ask me 3 questions, one at a time:

1. What ONE specific outcome would make this course a 10/10 for me?
2. What's my single biggest constraint (time, technical depth, team buy-in)?
3. What will I have built by July 19, 2026 that I don't have today?

After my answers, save to ~/.claude/course-goals.md (Codex: ~/.codex/course-goals.md).
Format: my answer to each question, plus a 1-line synthesis at the bottom.
Keep the whole file under 40 lines.
```

---

## 2 · goals-2026.md (5 min)

Paste this into your assistant:

```
Ask me three questions about 2026, one at a time:

1. What's ONE professional outcome I want to be true on Dec 31, 2026?
2. What's ONE personal outcome I want to be true?
3. What's ONE thing I want to STOP doing this year?

For each: ask a one-line follow-up about why it matters and what
the observable metric is.

Save to ~/.claude/goals-2026.md (Codex: ~/.codex/goals-2026.md).
Format per goal: outcome · why · observable metric (1 line each).
Keep the whole file under 40 lines.
```

---

## 3 · achievements.md (5-10 min)

Paste this into your assistant:

```
Ask me to list 5-10 wins from the last 3 years — anything I'm proud of,
professional or personal. Don't filter; if I include something small,
keep it.

For each win, ask a one-line follow-up: what made it possible?

Save to ~/.claude/achievements.md (Codex: ~/.codex/achievements.md).
Format per win: title · date · what made it possible (1 line each).
```

---

## Verify all 6 files exist

```bash
ls -la ~/.claude/*.md   # Codex: ls -la ~/.codex/*.md
```

You should see:

- `CLAUDE.md`  (or `AGENTS.md`)
- `SOUL.md`
- `user-profile.md`
- `course-goals.md`
- `goals-2026.md`
- `achievements.md`

## Test that Robin reloaded them

In a new session, ask Robin:

> Robin, tell me three things about how I work — and one risk in my CLAUDE.md.

If his answer surprises you, **Robin grew this week.** That's the W1 → W2 test question.
